How To Play

  • Learn the Basics

    Match cards in ascending or descending order to clear the board. Draw new cards from the deck when you’re out of moves.

  • Use Magical Boosters

    Wild Cards:
    Use these to match any card on the board.
    Power Spells:
    Clear multiple cards at once to overcome difficult layouts.

  • Complete Quests

    Each level comes with specific objectives, such as clearing a certain number of cards or uncovering hidden artifacts.

  • Unlock New Chapters

    Progress through the magical story by completing levels. Unlock new locations, characters, and challenges.

  • Master Special Cards

    Discover unique cards, like frozen or locked ones, that require strategy and magical powers to unlock.
